A List of The Worst Parts

A full list of some of the worst things to happen to be in 2020

* A Note: Sorry to be depressing, but that’s how the year’s been going.*

  1. I went to Europe where I had crippling depression due to missing my friends from home and hating the new people I had befriended.
  2. One of these people I befriended turned out to be extremely mean and made me cry on multiple occasions.
  3. One of the boys in the friend group was extremely racist and would get mad at me every time I called him out for being racist.
  4. I was put in a semester-long group project with a boy who would mansplain our project to me incorrectly and then proceed to not help with any of the work.
  5. My dream internship at an advertising agency in Chicago was cancelled due to coronavirus.
  6. I was kicked out of my abroad program due to coronavirus and sent back to the United States.
  7. When I arrived home, I discovered I had contracted coronavirus and had to quarantine alone for two weeks.
  8. I nannied for two devil children over the summer who made me cry a minimum of once a week. These children also made me never want to have kids.
  9. I arrived at college and discovered all of my classes were asynchronous and online, meaning I wouldn’t get to see any of my class friends for my senior year of college.
  10. Due to the lack of classes and in-person interaction, my depression worsened.
  11. I failed my first finance exam receiving a 42% which was then curved down to a 21%.

Overall I’d give 2020 a 0/20.
